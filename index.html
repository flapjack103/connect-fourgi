    About

    This game follows the traditional rules of connect four. The first player to get four in a row horizontally, vertically, or diagonally wins the game.

    To drop a chip, simply click anywhere on the column of your choice. You cannot drop chips into full columns.

    This page will keep track of the number of games each player has won. To restart mid-game, hit 'restart'.

    To change player names, double click on the name of the player to enter a new name.

    Enjoy!
